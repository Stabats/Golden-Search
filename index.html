Golden Search

   A plot based ARPG group about (maybe) saving the world. It is a set end roleplay with game play aspects such as currency, quests, crafting and more. Players will have 1 character to play in the world that will interact with the group mechanics and story. Characters will be able to influence the plot as the story progresses along with interact with the NPCs, world and create. Inspirations are Animal Crossing, Paradise Campaigns, LEGO Monkie Kid.

   Characters will be from whatever world the Player desires, however, no matter what world they hail from, things aren't quite right... Natural disasters have changed to become common and much more destructive with a magical nature. Random things such as items or even whole species have begun to disappear in thin air in each world with no cause and no return. Disappearances will either annoyance of losing belongings to disrupting ecosytems entirely. Each world is changing but for the worse. No matter what is happening in your world, it is clear that the natural order is disrupted as each world heads to the end of everything. You will hear about a place of myth called Talliah'la Island. Talliah'la Island is shrouded in myserty throughout history and common place to be used in tales of any kind, however, it is the universally agreed upon location of the ancient artifact: The Golden Key. The Golden Key known to hold the greatest powerful magic in the entire universe so powerful that it could actually fix the world. Perhaps you decide to seek out the Key to be the hero of your world or simply seek it out for other means... either way, you find yourself at a meeting that results in your arrival... somewhere not from your world. Oh, and you're a cat now.
